The Best Movies According to Kayla

My top 3 movies of all-time

Tangled

If I could do a top 10 movies, Disney would be king.
And need we say more about Tangled? It's a classic comfort movie.

Coco

It might seem like a simple coming of age/lesson learned movie
but it also includes an accurate portrayal of how music can help
people with memory impairment.

The Sound of Music

It's an interesting story told through music and beautiful visuals.
Family friendly all the way through.
